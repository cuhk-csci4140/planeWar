Game Play:
1. Get to the mobile phone entry point by scanning the QR code
2. You will need to calibrate your sensors for the first time
3. Type in the room number and your name(optional) then press "Connect"
4. Choose your plane
5. After everyone is ready, press start game button on this page
6. Fight until the last man standing
7. press restart game to restart the game (if you can't see your name/player X in the main screen, press the reconnect button on your phone)
